Article 1 — Scope. This regulation applies to systems that process personal data
and to products placed on the market that include automated decision-making.

Article 2 — Transparency. Providers shall make available clear documentation
describing the system's intended purpose and its known limitations.

Article 3 — Record-keeping. Providers shall keep logs sufficient to trace the
system's behaviour for a period appropriate to its risk.
